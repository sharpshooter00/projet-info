📖 The Story of Minecraft

In 2009, a Swedish programmer named Markus "Notch" Persson released a blocky game prototype. It started as a passion project, but it sparked a revolution. Players could build, destroy, explore, and survive in a limitless pixel world.

From humble caves to massive cities, Minecraft became more than a game — it became a *sandbox for imagination*. Now owned by Microsoft, it's one of the best-selling games ever. But real ones know: the magic began in **Alpha**. 💚

💡 Tips for Survival Mode

  • 🌑 Build a shelter before your first night!
  • 🪓 Use wood tools early, but get stone ASAP.
  • ⛏️ Dig at Y=11 to find diamonds (but don’t dig straight down!)
  • 🌿 Use beds to skip night & avoid phantoms.
