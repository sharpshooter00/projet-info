The Story of Minecraft

Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson and first released in 2009. It quickly became iconic for its sandbox style and pixel look. After being acquired by Microsoft, it became one of the best-selling games ever. Players build, survive, and explore infinite blocky worlds.

How to Play Minecraft

  • Chop trees, punch dirt, survive your first night.
  • Craft a shelter, mine stone, avoid creepers!
  • Explore biomes, fight mobs, collect gear.
  • Use WASD to move, click to attack/build, and E for inventory.
